10:00 - December 15, 2018 Conference Hall, Federal Neuro Psychiatric Hospital, Yaba, Lagos
Bringing together key individuals in the football world. A platform enabling and fostering connections between footballers, from grassroots to professional levels. A place to discuss, share ideas, and learn the ropes from the professionals in the industry. Building the foundation for a culture of mentorship and motivation in the football industry. Information that no one shares about how to make it through trials, how the language barrier affects chances, weather differences, and other pertinent factors.
At the heart of Footballers Connect 2018 is an open unconference-style event in which everyone who attends is welcome to contribute on any relevant topic. During these sessions, participants are free to interact, offer thoughts and experience, and share ideas and questions. These unconference sessions are also augmented with a series of presentations from current professionals, ex-internationals, in-the-field administrators, panel debates, and networking opportunities.
